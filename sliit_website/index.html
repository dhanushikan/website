RESEARCH DOMAIN

Sri Lanka‚Äôs coastal wind farms, such as Thambapavani in Mannar, face challenges like yaw misalignment, cut-in/cut-out shutdowns, noise pollution, and unpredictable lightning risks. Traditional monitoring methods are reactive, leading to unplanned downtime, higher costs, and reduced community acceptance.

Most Digital Twin applications in wind energy are limited to single components (like fault detection or power curves) and rely on offshore datasets. There is a lack of holistic, real-time frameworks tailored for tropical coastal conditions with localized validation.
